Coin Rush is an exciting 2D platformer game where you control a pirate who runs through various levels collecting gold coins and avoiding enemies. The objective is to reach the end of the level and grab your pirate hat to win! However, the journey is full of danger, as there are gaps where the pirate can fall and pirate enemies who will try to stop him.

The game includes a health bar, so the pirate must avoid damage from enemies and falling into pits. The game also features a range of immersive sound effects that enhance the experience, from the sounds of coins being collected to the pirate's footsteps and enemy attacks.

Game Features

  • Health Bar: The pirate has a health bar that depletes when he gets hit by enemies or falls into pits.
  • Gold Collection: The pirate collects gold coins scattered throughout the levels, adding an extra incentive to explore and jump across platforms.
  • Enemy Pirates: Pirate enemies roam the levels and will attack the player on sight. Players must avoid or defeat them to stay alive.
  • Level Objective: To win the level, the pirate must collect his hat, which is located at the end of the level. Collecting the hat marks the level as complete and allows the player to proceed to the next one.
  • Interactive Platforms: Some platforms move, disappear, or require the player to time jumps and actions precisely.
  • Sound Effects: The game includes immersive sound effects such as footsteps, coin collection sounds, enemy attacks, and environmental sounds, making the experience feel dynamic and engaging.

Tech Stack

The following technologies were used to develop Coin Rush:

  • Python: Python was used as the primary programming language for game logic, mechanics, and event handling.
  • Pygame: Pygame was used for handling the game's graphical rendering, input management, and event handling. It provides the necessary tools to create the platformer mechanics.
  • Aseprite: Aseprite was used to design the pixel art sprites, including the pirate character, enemies, platforms, and backgrounds.
  • Tiled: Tiled was used to design the levels, create tile maps, and structure obstacles and platforms within the game.

Development Process

The development of Coin Rush started with basic platformer mechanics, including the pirate’s movement, jumping, and gravity mechanics. The initial focus was on getting the player’s movement fluid and responsive. From there, we added interactive platforms and obstacles, followed by the creation of the health bar and the coin collection system.

After the basic mechanics were in place, we began implementing the enemy pirates, who could damage the player. Each enemy was given unique behavior and movement patterns. The levels were designed using the Tiled level editor, making it easier to create varied and interesting environments.

The art assets, including the pirate character, enemies, platforms, and backgrounds, were created using Aseprite. The game’s pixel art style helps to give it a classic, retro feel.

Gameplay Mechanics

Coin Rush features a simple but engaging control system. The player controls the pirate using keyboard input, navigating platforms, avoiding enemies, and collecting gold coins. The core mechanics include:

  • Player Movement: The pirate can run, jump, and fall, with realistic gravity influencing movement and jump height.
  • Health System: The pirate has a health bar that decreases when hit by enemies or falls into pits. If the health bar reaches zero, the player must restart the level.
  • Gold Coins: Coins are scattered throughout the level, and the player must collect them to increase their score. Coins also serve as an incentive to explore different areas of each level.
  • Enemies: Pirate enemies patrol the levels and will attack the player on sight. Players need to either avoid them or find ways to neutralize them.
  • Level Completion: To win the level, the player must reach the end of the level and collect their pirate hat, which is placed in a designated spot.

Challenges Faced

One of the major challenges during development was ensuring that the pirate's movement felt natural and responsive, while also adding complexity with interactive platforms and different enemy types. The health bar system needed fine-tuning to ensure it provided meaningful feedback to the player while maintaining the right balance of difficulty.

Another challenge was designing levels that were engaging but not too difficult. Playtesting was key to balancing the platforming puzzles and enemy encounters, as well as ensuring that the game remained fun without becoming frustrating.

Future Improvements

There are several features planned for future updates of Coin Rush:

  • Power-ups: Introduce power-ups like temporary invincibility or super jumps to add an additional layer of strategy and excitement.
  • New Levels: Create new levels with different environments, enemies, and obstacles to increase the variety of the gameplay experience.
  • Boss Fights: Add boss battles at the end of certain levels to increase difficulty and provide a more epic conclusion to each stage.
  • Soundtrack: Enhance the game’s audio with a full soundtrack that complements the gameplay and adds immersion.
